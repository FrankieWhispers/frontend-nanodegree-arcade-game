frontend-nanodegree-arcade-game
===============================

How to play:
1. Open with your favorite browser.
2. Use arrow keys to move from grass area to the water without being captured by a bug.
3. Receive 5 points and reset to grass area for making it to the water.
4. Receive -10 points and sent back to the grass area if captured by a bug.
5. Try to get a high score before you get to sleepy to continue on.

Enjoy!!!!

*Don’t cheat by refreshing your browser if your score gets too low. I know your type.
